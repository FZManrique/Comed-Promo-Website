Mindanao State University

College of Education Training Department

MSU-CETD is one of the departments of MSU-GSC, its vision is To be a globally-competitive university in Southern Philippines and its mission is To provide competent human resources for the development of Southern Mindanao and to help improve the living conditions of Muslims and Indigenous People. MSU-CETD is also cheap, making it accessible to most people, The teachers are strict but also good at teaching us students.

MSU-CETD is also known for the numerous events they host for the students. For the current school year 2022-2023, here's a glance on some events held by the institution:

  1. TEACHERS' DAY (October 25, 2022)

    The SSG decided to organize a simple celebration for Teachers' Day that was filled with entertaining activities such as a band performance, dance presentations, and a gift giving segment. Each class also held a separate program in each of their classrooms for their advisers and teachers. It was surely a day to remember.

  2. SCI-MATH DAY (October 27, 2022)

    The school makes it a habit to celebrate the Sci-Math Day. The students were divided into three different houses with their own yells and chants. Students were also able to join in the quiz bowl and dance competition. Amazing is an understatement to describe the event.

  3. INTRAMURALS (November 28-December 1, 2022)

    Make way for the most anticipated event, the Intramurals! The students showed their talents and spirit of sportsmanship through activities like zumba, color run, and outdoor and indoor games. The weeklong celebration ended with the students' night, which the students enjoyed with their friends and classmates.

These events do not stop here! Coming up next is the MAPEH and Diversity Day for the month of February 2023. Some events like the Family Day and English Day are also on the works. Stay tuned for more updates!
