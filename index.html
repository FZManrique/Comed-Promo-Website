Mindanao State University

College of Education Training Department

MSU-CETD is one of the departments of MSU-GSC, its vision is To be a globally-competitive university in Southern Philippines and its mission is To provide competent human resources for the development of Southern Mindanao and to help improve the living conditions of Muslims and Indigenous People. MSU-CETD is also cheap, making it accessible to most people, The teachers are strict but also good at teaching us students.

MSU-CETD is also known for the numerous events they host for the students. For the current school year 2022-2023, here's a glance on some events held by the institution:

  1. TEACHERS' DAY

    Last October 5, 2022, the SSG decided to organize a simple celebration for Teachers' Day. The day was filled with entertaining events. Students displayed their talents as a simple present for their teachers. The Grade 10 students performed a band, while the Grades 7, 8 and 9 showed their dancing prowess. The advisers and subject teachers received gifts from the students. Each sections gathered in their classrooms to celebrate together with their classmates. They prepared a small surprise for their adviser and brought foods to share. The day was surely enjoyable for both the students and teachers.

  2. SCI-MATH DAY

    Last year, on the 27th of October, the high school celebrated Sci-Math Day! Students and teachers were having fun. They're shouting their yells and chants. Solving equations and answering simple questions is one of the events in Sci-Math Day. Some students from different houses express their talent in dancing and singing. The students and also the teachers surely enjoyed the amazing event.

  3. INTRAMURALS

    Last year, from November 28 to December 1, we had our most anticipated intramurals. It was a week of spectacular events. We had physical activities like zumba, a color fun run, and outdoor and indoor games. Students showed their talents and spirit of sportsmanship. The weeklong celebration ended with the students' night, which the students enjoyed with their friends and classmates.

These events do not stop here! Coming up next is the MAPEH and Diversity Day for the month of February 2023. Some events like the Family Day and English Day are also on the works. Stay tuned for more updates!
